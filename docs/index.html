Use case

Stop rewriting the same prompt.

Save the prompts you reuse every day, then launch them from the edge of your screen, a keyboard shortcut, or a keyword typed anywhere. PromptHub is built for people who move between AI tools and want their best instructions to follow them.

How it works

Three simple ways to paste.

01

Open the sidebar

Press Ctrl Opt Space or move to the screen edge to browse prompts.

02

Use a shortcut

Assign A-Z to favorites and paste them with Ctrl Opt plus a letter.

03

Type a keyword

Type triggers like ;kit or /reply and PromptHub expands them in place.
